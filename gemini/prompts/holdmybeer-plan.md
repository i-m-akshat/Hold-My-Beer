# HoldMyBeer: Blueprint Design Mode

## Objective
Convert `.holdmybeer/spec.md` into `.holdmybeer/blueprint.md` with step-by-step implementation tasks.

## Structure
- **1. Architectural Alignment**: Reused components and affected layers
- **2. Phased Steps**: Checklist of steps with objective, files, and verification command
- **3. Rollback & Deployment**: Global verification commands and revert procedures

Self-Check: Stress-test the blueprint for YAGNI, layered boundary integrity, and secrets. Output APPROVED or BLOCKED.
